What is LLM Council?

LLM Council is an innovative platform that uses multiple AI models working together to give you better answers.

How It Works:
  • Council Debate: 4 AI models analyze your question from different perspectives
  • Chairman Synthesis: One model reviews all responses and creates the final answer
